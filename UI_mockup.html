PRIN 2.1.1R (Skill, Care and Diligence)
A firm must conduct its business with due skill, care and diligence. This applies to the design and operation of automated advice systems.
COBS 2.1.1R (Treating Customers Fairly)
A firm must act honestly, fairly and professionally in accordance with the best interests of its customer when providing investment services.
COBS 3.1.1R (Conflict of Interest)
A firm must identify conflicts of interest and take reasonable steps to manage them. Algorithmic bias in recommendations must be disclosed.
